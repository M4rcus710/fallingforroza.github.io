Our Story So Far

We are Falling For Roza, a newcomer rock band from Munich, and we’re here to make some noise. Everything we do is 100% DIY—from writing and recording to getting our music out there—and we pour everything we’ve got into every song we create. Our music is all about contrast: emotional lyrics meet hard-hitting riffs, but we’re not afraid to strip it back and get softer when the moment calls for it.

We just released our debut EP, Red Shirt, and it’s available everywhere now. It’s a raw, honest reflection of who we are and what we’re about—music that hits hard, sticks with you, and tells stories we hope you’ll connect with. This is just the beginning, and we’re excited to have you along for the ride. Let’s fall together.
