Our Story So Far

We are Falling For Roza, a newcomer rock band from Munich, and were here to make some noise. Everything we do is 100% DIYfrom writing and recording to getting our music out thereand we pour everything weve got into every song we create. Our music is all about contrast: emotional lyrics meet hard-hitting riffs, but were not afraid to strip it back and get softer when the moment calls for it.

We just released our debut EP, Red Shirt, and its available everywhere now. Its a raw, honest reflection of who we are and what were aboutmusic that hits hard, sticks with you, and tells stories we hope youll connect with. This is just the beginning, and were excited to have you along for the ride. Lets fall together.
